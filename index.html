Biography

I am a Senior researcher (Associate Professor) at the Department of Management Engineering in Technical University of Denmark (DTU). I am a member of the research group of Climate Change and Sustainable Development (CCSD) within the university.

I did my PhD at the Department of Computer Science (Daisy research group) in Aalborg University (AAU) Denmark from August 2008 to May 2012. Prior to joining AAU, I was a scientific assistant in the Åbo Akademi University in Finland from September 2006 to July 2008. During January 2013 to December 2014, I was a postdoctoral researcher in the research group of Information System and Science Energy (ISS4E) at University of Waterloo, Canada, and at the same period, I was cross-appointed as a research scientist by IBM Toronto research center.

Research Interests

I am interested in Smart Meter Data Analytics, Data Management (in particular, data warehousing), Big Data, and Data Mining. My current research includes:

  • Smart meter data analytics
    • Benchmarking smart meter data analytics systems and algorithms
    • Smart meter Data analytics System
    • Anomaly energy consumption detection
  • Energy Informatics
  • Big data management, and ETL
  • Internet of Things

Open Source Software

I developed the following open source software systems in my previous research work (Github: https://github.com/xiufengliu):

  1. SEGSys: An online mapping system for segmentation analysis of energy consumption.
  2. CATool: A ready-to-use experiment tool for climate researchers to understand the pitfalls in adaptation decisions in behavioral economics
  3. IoTDashboard: This project is to create the dashboard that can collect indoor and outdoor climate data, and visualize the time series in a real time.
  4. K-SC Clustering Algorithm on Spark
  5. DataGenerator-Cluster-Version:A scalable smart meter data generator on Spark
  6. CITIESData: A scalable smart city data management platform
  7. SMAS: A smart meter data analytics system
  8. CloudETL: A Scalable Dimensional ETL for Hive
  9. BigETL: A unified ETL platform for supporting various data processing technologies, including Spark, Hive, Hadoop, Python, Linux Shell script, etc.
  10. 3XL: An efficient OWL/RDF Triple Store Supporting Bulk Operations
  11. ETLMR: A Python-based Dimensional ETL Framework based on MapReduce (Obsolete)
  12. All-RiTE: A Right-time ETL middle-ware Supporting Insert, Update and Delete Operations (not released yet)
