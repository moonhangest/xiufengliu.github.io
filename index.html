Biography

I am a Senior researcher at the Department of Management Engineering in Technical University of Denmark (DTU). I am a member of the research group of Climate Change and Sustainable Development (CCSD) within the university.

I did my PhD at the Department of Computer Science (Daisy research group) in Aalborg University (AAU) Denmark from August 2008 to May 2012. Prior to joining AAU, I was a scientific assistant in the Åbo Akademi University in Finland from September 2006 to July 2008. During January 2013 to December 2014, I was a postdoctoral researcher in the research group of Information System and Science Energy (ISS4E) at University of Waterloo, Canada, and at the same period I was co-appointed as a research scientist by IBM Toronto research center.

I obtained my Master and Bachelor degrees of Computer Science from Software School of Tsinghua university in China in 2005 and 2001, respectively.

Research Interests

I am interested in Smart Meter Data Analytics, Data Management (in particular, data warehousing), Big Data, and Data Mining. My current research includes:

  • Smart meter data analytics
    • Benchmarking smart meter data analytics systems and algorithms
    • Smart meter Data analytics System
    • Anomaly energy consumption detection
  • Energy Informatics
  • Big data management, and ETL
  • Internet of Things

Open Source Software

I developed the following open source software systems in my previous research works (Github: https://github.com/xiufengliu):

  1. CATool: A ready-to-use experiment tool for climate researchers to understand the pitfalls in adaptation decisions in behavioral economics
  2. IoTDashboard: This project is to create the dashboard that can collect indoor and outdoor climate data, and visualize the time series in a real time.
  3. K-SC Clustering Algorithm on Spark
  4. DataGenerator-Cluster-Version:A scalable smart meter data generator on Spark
  5. CITIESData: A scalable smart city data management platform
  6. SMAS: A smart meter data analytics system
  7. CloudETL: A Scalable Dimensional ETL for Hive
  8. BigETL: A unified ETL platform of supporting various data processing technologies, including Spark, Hive, Hadoop, Python, Linux Shell script, etc.
  9. 3XL:An efficient OWL/RDF Triple Store Supporting Bulk Operations
  10. ETLMR: A Python-based Dimensional ETL Framework based on MapReduce (Obsolete)
  11. All-RiTE: A Right-time ETL middle-ware Supporting Insert, Update and Delete Operations (not released yet)
