Biography

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Management Engineering at Technical University of Denmark. I am a member of the research group of Climate Change and Sustainable Development (CCSD) within the university.

I did my PhD study in Department of Computer Science, Aalborg University (AAU) Denmark from August 2008 to May 2012, under the supervision of Prof. Torben Bach Pedersen. Prior to joining AAU, I was a scientific assistant in the Aboakademi University in Finland from September 2006 to July 2008. During January 2013 to December 2014, I was a postdoctoral researcher in the research group of Information System and Science Energy at University of Waterloo, Canada, and at the same period I was co-appointed as a research scientist by IBM Toronto research center.

I obtained my Master and Bachelor degrees of Computer Science from Tsinghua university, China, in 2005 and 2001, respectively.

Research Interests

I am interested in Smart Meter Data Analytics, Data Management (in particular, data warehousing), Big Data, and Data Mining. My current research includes:

  • Smart meter data analytics
    • Benchmarking smart meter data analytics systems and algorithms (EDBT15, ICDE15)
    • Smart meter Data analytics System (SDEWES15, Energy)
    • Anomaly energy consumption detection (TODS)
  • Big data management, and ETL (IDEA15, DaWak11)
  • Sensor data cleansing (ADBIS15)
